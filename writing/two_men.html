two men

writing prompt: two men walk into a bar at exactly 5pm on a tuesday.

    How many times have you heard the joke, two people walk into a bar, “Knock Knock”, but as it happened on the screen in
    front of them they could hardly believe their eyes, it would be unbearably funny if it wasnt unfeasably sad, they stood
    there watching as 14½ million dollars worth of equipment was escorted in to a truck by two people who by all accounts
    would have fit in perfectly well with Curly, Larry and Moe Howard. Kennedy considered saying “why I outta” but he
    decided against it figuring the mood was so dark not even the headlights of a car could brighten them. Truthfully it
    probably would have had no effect at all as by this time Reggie was already deep in his thinking pose which famously
    took two full buckets of ice water dumped on his head to remove him from if he had not yet fully pondered this particular
    contemplation. 

    At this point he had determined a few things conclusively, first the 2 Stooges on the monitor in front of him were almost
    certainly not the ones behind this plan, they looked like common crooks, and acted like common thugs, not only would they
    not have been able to formulate a plan to get into such a highly obfuscated building but they wouldn’t even have thought to,
    there were no public records of this machine, if there were, they never released any valuation and quite frankly no one
    had much use for it other than them. No, this attack was masterminded by someone else and it was targeted, every detail had
    been planned out intricately the security panels on the doors had been hacked, the rooms were quickly navigated so a
    floor plan was clearly used… It was at this point something very peculiar happened in Reggie’s mind as he found himself
    coming back to a thought after it had passed, Reggie prided himself very deeply on his ability to fully comprehend
    everything on a first pass and so this feeling was highly unusual, however it was important enough that it bypassed any
    elitist pride that may otherwise have stopped him. If the attackers had access to the security system then they had
    access to the cameras, the attacker wanted them to see this, this attacker had likely purposefully sent the 2 most
    bumbling idiots they could just to toy with them. 

    They were taunting him yelling at him “A couple of circus clowns can bypass your security.” This was beyond personal.
    Reggie and Kennedy’s time in the industry had earned them somewhat of a reputation, a nickname of sorts ‘Compound
    Interest’, not a particularly good nickname mind you but they begrudgingly grew to respect it. The idea was quite simple
    if ever they held a debt they would pay it back tenfold, an idea thought up by Kennedy that his partner in crime often
    believed was the smartest he’d ever had, through this system they quickly grew trust in communities and established
    themselves as honorable men who anyone worth their salts would want to partner with. Their policy came with an
    additional stipulation however, any indiscretion against them, any slight at their business or their peoples. That too
    would be repayed tenfold. This had a very similair impact of getting others on their good side, although with very
    different motivation. As far as Kennedy and Reggie were concerned this was quite the indescretion, and they intended to
    be generous when repaying.
